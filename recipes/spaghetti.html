Spaghetti Bolognese

A rich, delicious, traditional spaghetti bolognese that will really impress your partner! So simple yet so yummy!

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoon oliv oil
  • 3 large onions
  • 2 pounds lean ground beef
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 cup red wine

Preparation

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water, then drain and set aside.
  2. Cook the beef on the stove and discard the grease. Transfer to a bowl and mix in the spaghetti sauce.
  3. Cover with remaining spaghetti and butter.
  4. Pour the beef mixture over the spaghetti and spread in an even layer.
  5. Bake for 30 minutes, top with Cheese, and continue baking until the cheese is melted.
